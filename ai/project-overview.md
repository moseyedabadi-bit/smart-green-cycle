# Smart Green Cycle - AI Project Overview

## Project Name

Smart Green Cycle

## Project Category

Circular Economy
Sustainable Retail
ESG
Reverse Logistics

## Problem

Retail chains spend significant resources on disposable shopping bags that create environmental impact while generating no long-term customer value.

## Proposed Solution

A return-and-reward framework where customers return shopping bags and receive incentives through loyalty systems.

Returned bags enter a controlled recycling stream and generate environmental and economic value.

## Core Principles

* Circular Economy
* Behavioral Economics
* Customer Retention
* Reverse Logistics
* ESG Alignment

## Target Organizations

* Retail Chains
* Supermarkets
* FMCG Distribution Networks
* Sustainability Programs

## Expected Outcomes

* Reduced plastic waste
* Increased customer retention
* Improved ESG performance
* Creation of recyclable material streams
* Reduction of operational waste costs
